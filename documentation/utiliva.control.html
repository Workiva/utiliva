utiliva.control

Control forms.

->>?->

macro

(->>?-> expr & clauses)
Like ?->, but the conditions are threaded with ->> and the results are
threaded with ->.
(->>?-> 4 (> 2) inc (> 6) dec)

->?->>

macro

(->?->> expr & clauses)
Like cond->, but threads the argument through the conditions as well.
(->?->> 4 (> 2) inc (> 6) dec)

?->

macro

(?-> expr & clauses)
Like cond->, but threads the argument through the conditions as well.
(?-> 4 even? inc odd? inc neg? inc)

?->>

macro

(?->> expr & clauses)
Like cond->>, but threads the argument through the conditions as well.
(?->> 4 even? (assoc {} :four))
